Processing information - Chunk Size
This model relates to the size of the information chunks or pieces people are comfortable dealing with. People who are sorting globally get bored with details whereas a specific person gets frustrated with overviews and summaries.
Relationship Filrer - Matching/Mismatching
The relationship filter is also known as the sameness difference filter or matching mismatching. It influences how we compare information. With sameness or matching, we search for what things or situations have in common. With difference or mismatching, w e notice how things are dissimilar. The effect on personality can be dramatic. Do you know someone, who no matter what you say seems to disagree? When you suggest it is white and they insist it is black?
Representational Sort - Visual/Auditory
The representational sort filter exhibit our preferred way to represent external data in our mind. When we think about something do you visualize it first in your mind, or process it with sounds ? Does it look great or does it sound good ?
Reality structure - Static/process
This model represent our preferred way of thinking about “reality” according to you. Do you think about reality in term of something permanent, solid, fixed (ex : it is the way I am, It is the way the world is) or as something in consent process, changing, evolving
Attribution Style - Best Case / Worst case scenario
Attribution style exhibit our prefered way at looking at challenging situation. Do we define it as problems, difficulties that need a fix or a solution before been a threat, or as an opportunity that could lead to a positive outcome.
Perceptual categories sort - Binary vs shades
Perceptual category sort represent our way to filter a situation or an information as binary, broad categories ( good and bad, black and white, best-worst) or in a more ganual way, with grey areas in between (better than, worst than)
Frame of reference - Internal/External
The frame of reference models exhibit the way we evaluate a person, situation, an experience or an idea. Do you rely mostly on yourself ? Or do you need external validation. Do Do you know when you have done a great job ? Or do you need validation from your peers?
Motivation Direction - Away from/ Toward
Motivation direction refer to the the direction you generally take in terms of motivational strategy. Do you move toward what you want to achieve or do you tend to go away from the things you want to avoid. Do you go to the gym in order to be healthier or do you go to the gym to not gain weight.
Preferences filter - People/Place/Things-Activity/Information
Preferences refers to your primary focus regarding your interests or your involvement in a project. Would you rather be interested in the people ? The place ? The things that you would be able to do ? or what you will learn ?

Action filter - Active/Reactive
Action filter exhibit your behaviour preference when you come into a situation that require action. Do you usually act quickly after sizing up, or do you prefer to take some, assess the situation and the possible outcome before rushing into it.
Management Filter - Management/ Independent worker/ Dependent worker/ Potential Manager

Management filter exhibit your prefered style for handling the experience of working with other people in a task oriented situation.

  • A Manager style will know what he needs, what other need in order to be more successful and will have the confidence to express it.
  • An independent worker will know what he needs to be more successful but won’t know what other should need as well.
  • A dependant worker won’t exactly know what he need to be successful and would need supervision
  • A potential manager knows what he needs and what other needs but don’t have the authority to express it.
Choice filters - Process/Options

Choice filter exhibit the preferred style for dealing with instructions or for getting something done. Do you prefer to keep to keep you options open and do it in the order that feels right to you ? Or do you prefer to follow a step by step procedure ?

Goal planning - Perfection/Optimizing

Goal planning sort refers to your prefered style for relating to goals. If you set a goal to accomplish something of importance how would you handle it ? Would you strive to perfection for you and others or would you be more interest in optimizing the process ?

Information gathering filter - Sensor/Intuitor

Information gathering filters refers to your prefered way to gather information to reach a decision. Do you prefer to gather information to external data, pictures, numbers, feedbacks or do you prefer to go “insider” and trust your intuition ?

Locus of control filter - Internal/ external locus of control

Locus of control refers to you prefered style for explaining the “cause” of an event or an experience. Do you believe that you are the only responsible for what us happening to you or do you believe that some part was influenced by external variables such as luck, destiny, background.

Temper to instruction - Strong will/ Compliant

Temper to instruction exhibit your prefered response when confronting someone telling you something. Even if you do what is asked will you think about whether it is the best way to do it, or will it bring value, or are you the right person to do it (think & execute) .. or will you directly comply with the ask ?

Time filters - In time/ Through time

Time fiters exhibit your preferred way of experiencing time. As you recall events in your life in the past can you see those memories in front of you and sequence them from left to right after before (Through time) or would you locate those memories kind of behind you, having more difficulties to reach them. Though time people tend to be more organized and time conscious. In time people tend to live for the moment and are less time conscious

Knowledge Source Sort - Modeling/Conceptualizing/Demonstrations/Experiencing

The knowledge source filter refers to your prefered way to learn new things or skills: Do you prefer to:

  • Do you learn by yourself, by studying, researching going back to your books trying to dissect the how the why the what in each topic (Conceptualizing)
  • Do you learn by yourself from others, in other word by trying to study how expert become the best in their field and by apply those techniques you will eventually become a master as well; (Modeling)
  • Do you learn the most when you are teached by a coach, a mentor, a professor, it gives you motivation, structure and make you learning in a much faster pace.(demonstration)
  • Do you learn the most by actually doing it, testing, practicing.
